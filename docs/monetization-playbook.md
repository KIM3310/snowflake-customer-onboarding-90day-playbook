# Monetization Playbook - snowflake-customer-onboarding-90day-playbook

This is a commercial planning artifact, not a revenue guarantee. It exists to turn an archived technical proof into a clear, ethical selling motion.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** Snowflake onboarding and customer success playbook
- **Primary buyer:** Snowflake partners, data platform teams, customer success leaders, and system integrators.
- **Why this can sell:** The first 90 days after contract signature decide adoption, cost trust, governance posture, and long-term expansion.

## Offer Ladder

1. **90-day onboarding audit** - $5k-$18k. Assess discovery, landing zone, governance, and success-review readiness.
2. **Onboarding acceleration package** - $25k-$100k. Customize templates and SQL scripts for one customer rollout.
3. **Partner enablement license** - $2k-$15k/month. Maintain reusable onboarding templates, scripts, and success-review material.

## Acquisition Channels

- Snowflake partner content
- Data platform onboarding guides
- Cross-sell from demo-pack engagements

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- Templates are complete and easy to adapt
- Governance and cost controls are prominent
- Scripts are clearly marked as starting points

## Risk Boundaries

- Do not imply Snowflake endorsement
- Avoid guaranteeing business outcomes without baseline data
- Keep customer-specific implementations private

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish the 90-day readiness checklist
- **60 days:** Create a sample onboarding executive review
- **90 days:** Package templates as a partner enablement offer

## Metrics That Matter

- Audit bookings
- Template reuse
- Time-to-first-value
- Partner renewals

## Positioning Note

Keep the repository's archived status visible. Sell the reusable workflow, diagnostic, enablement material, or pilot path that the proof makes credible; do not sell the archive as an unsupported production service.
